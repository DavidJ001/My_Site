Who is David Joel?

David Joel is a multifaceted artist and developer. His skills range from graphic design and video editing to illustration and web programming. A creative problem solver, with an eye for detail and a desire to bring ideas to life. He is a computer science student with a passion for software engineering. He believes he is one of the few who can still get excited about coding. His goal is to become a world class developer and as such, he is always looking for ways.

He is also a mental health activist and a certified member of the American Association of Christian Counselors (AACC). He believes it's vital to maintain good mental health as a software engineer.

Her's why: Programming can drive a software developer insane because it requires a lot of thought and focus. Unfortunately, many software developers are so immersed in their work that they are unaware of the impact this intense cognitive activity can have on their mental health.
